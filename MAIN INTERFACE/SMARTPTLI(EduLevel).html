    Anakku Pintar

    This program is specifically designed for helping young learners master the basics of the alphabet and foundational literacy skills through engaging and supportive teaching methods.

    Primary School

    This program offers a comprehensive curriculum that emphasizes the fundamentals of learning, including language, mathematics, and science, to build a strong academic foundation for young learners.

    Secondary School

    This program provides students with advanced learning opportunities in core subjects, preparing them for higher education and future career paths with a focus on critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

    SPM Classes

    This program is tailored for students preparing for their Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia (SPM) examinations, offering intensive lessons, practice exams, and personalized coaching to help them excel in their studies.

    Smart Iqra & Al-Quran

    This program are dedicated to teaching students the art of reading and understanding the Quran, incorporating lessons on Islamic values and practices to enhance their spiritual growth.

    KAFA Classes

    This program provides Islamic education for students, focusing on Fardu Ain, Akhlaq, and basic Quranic studies, ensuring that they gain knowledge and understanding of Islamic teachings in a structured manner.
